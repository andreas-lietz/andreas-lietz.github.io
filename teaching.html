Informal Seminar

I run an informal Casual Set Theory Seminar, currently via Zoom wednesdays at 1pm c.t.. At the moment we discuss the structure of the real line in terms of Cichon's Diagram. Anyone interested is welcome! Some knowledge on forcing is required to follow. If you would like to join us, please give me a heads up, for example via email.

Here you can find the exercises for the "Logik III" lecture, held by Ralf Schindler during WS 19/20.
